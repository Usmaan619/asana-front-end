Founder and CEO Maaz Memon

MAAZ MEMON

Maaz Memon has 14 years experience in the traditional banking world working for the likes of OKTA, projects in partnership with the Federal Reserve Bank, and HSBC where he led a $450 million dollar transformation program to help develop their Global Wallet system. His leadership abilities led Maaz to consult for and lead the creation of the first multi- currency payment platform for Expedia. Through his experience in building out projects for major institutions, Maaz realized where there is a gap in emerging blockchain technology and the traditional financial system, and thus, BHAI Finance was born. 

For more information about Maaz, visit www.maazmemon.com
